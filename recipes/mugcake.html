Mug Cake

by congerdesign via pixabay

Description

This is an easy recipe to create an fluffy chocolate cake in your microwave

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 tablespoon neutral oil
  • optional: chocolate chips

Steps

  1. mix flour, sugar, cocoa and salt in a mug
  2. add oil and milk and mix well
  3. put the mug in the microwave on high power for a maximum of 50 (depending on how you like it)
