Pizza Dough

by Skitterphoto via pixabay

This is my favorite recipe for pizza dough. It's fast and easy.

Ingredients

  • 450 g flour
  • Â½ cube yeast, approx. 21 g
  • 1 pinch of sugar
  • 15 g salt
  • 12 ml olive oil
  • 250 ml warm water

Steps

  1. Mix the yeast, sugar, salt, olive oil and water well. add it to the flour and knead it into a smooth dough.
  2. Divide the dough into 4 portions, form them into balls.
  3. Leave them to rise for 2 - 3 hours, covered and lightly oiled.
